PVCBot - A Low-cost Marine Sampling Solution

Allows CTD measurements along predefined waypoints

See PVCBot in Action!

Accurate CTD Measurements

The custom-designed sensors onboard allow conductivity, temperature and depth measurements with high accuracy.

Accurate Navigation

The PVCBot comes with a EKF-enabled waypoint navigation system.

Stable Mechanical Design

The robot is designed to be stable under moderate weather conditions.

Low-Cost and Customizable

The entire solution costs less than a hundred dollars, and comes with easily-modified payload.

Low-drag Profile

The robot is designed to have minimal drag, allowing longer mission time.

Fresh and Salt

All electronics are specially designed to be corrosion-resistant.
